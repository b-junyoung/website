Miyuki Shirogane

The president of the student council and top student of the school. He's hardworking, diligent, and often seen as the model student. He harbors romantic feelings for Kaguya but struggles with expressing them due to his pride and fear of rejection.

Kaguya Shinomiya

The vice president of the student council and comes from a wealthy and prestigious family. She's intelligent, composed, and highly competitive. Like Miyuki, she has feelings for him but refuses to confess first, leading to various schemes and mind games to make him confess to her.

Chika Fujiwara

The secretary of the student council and known for her cheerful and airheaded personality. She brings a lighthearted and unpredictable element to the council. Chika is friends with both Miyuki and Kaguya and often unintentionally disrupts their schemes with her obliviousness.

Yu Ishigami

The treasurer of the student council and initially introduced as a gloomy and socially awkward student. He's incredibly intelligent but has a cynical outlook on life due to past experiences. Yu becomes a close friend of the main trio and plays a crucial role in their interactions and schemes.

Miko Iino

Introduced later in the series, Miko is initially a member of the disciplinary committee and later becomes the auditor of the student council. She is extremely principled, strict, and diligent in upholding rules and morals. Miko is known for her strong sense of justice and dedication to her duties, which sometimes puts her at odds with the other council members' more relaxed attitudes. Over time, she forms connections with the student council members and becomes an integral part of their group dynamics.
